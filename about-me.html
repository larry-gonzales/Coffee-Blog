My name is John and living the coffee life is my single passion

I have always loved the taste of coffee. I appreciate the taste of coffee without any creamers or sugar. I can’t tell you how excited I was when I began to be able to taste the difference between the different roasts and brands.

When you appreciate coffee for more than the caffeine it provides, that’s when you truly begin to love coffee.

Coffee helps me relax

Because coffee is so inviting, I think it’s just natural for me that I feel comforted and at ease when I can sit back and enjoy a cup. Like I have mentioned before, from its scent to its taste, it’s an enticing experience.

I am not a morning person at all, which means I wake up slowly. Coffee is a great way to ease me back to life, but keeps me feeling very relaxed at the same time.

Now I live the coffee life

Now, don’t get me wrong, I do love coffee for the caffeine it provides! Somedays coffee really is the only thing that can get me up and feeling like I can accomplish my dreams and goals.

It gives me the boost I need to stay focused and energized. It’s my midday pick-me-up that perks me up and keeps me fueled for the rest of the day.
